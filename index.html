Background check

My love for programming started at my very young age I was in my early years in High School, when i discovered how fascinating computers are when dealing with communication and information processing. I got more and more into computers as i moved up in classes and development levels as computers became available to me. I then decided to buy a personal computer of my own so i would have one readily available to me at all times.

I was curious as to what more i could do with my PC other than just some microsoft word and Corel drawing. I found out that computers can be given commands as to what to display on screen for audiences using not MS word, but a software that deals with codes and computer languages. I then decided that i would want to learn and know how that is done.

That is how i started my journey in learn basic HTML and CSS on my own while i carry on with my High School classes. To be honest, i could not wait to graduate from High School and learn this art for real in a university with good and available resources.

After my graduation i moved to the United States to further my education in Web design and development, i felt comfortable moving here as i was moving to study in a university belonging to my religion. Current a junior at the Brigham Young University-Idaho.
